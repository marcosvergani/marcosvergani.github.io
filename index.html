Contact Summary

This is the summary of the contact you just handled. This is meant to be automatically brought up upon contact completion. The idea is to save Agent's time by reducing after call work providing a text that they can just copy and paste, avoiding writting the summary of the contact on their own

You will be able to copy it with a click on the button below, and to paste it in your CRM by pressing CTRL+V (Windows) or CMND+V (MacOS).
